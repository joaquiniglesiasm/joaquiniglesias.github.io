What is Capture The Flag (CTF)?

Imagine a digital treasure hunt where you're the cyber detective. That's what a Capture The Flag (CTF) is all about:

  • You'll solve puzzles and overcome challenges related to computer security.
  • Each solved challenge reveals a "flag" - a secret piece of text that proves you cracked the code.
  • You might find yourself decrypting messages, finding hidden data, or safely navigating computer systems.
  • CTFs can be solo adventures or team quests, perfect for both personal growth and team bonding.
